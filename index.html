HOW TO CREATE RISO ANIMATIONS

Creating animations with the risograph printer has become a hobby and obsession of Alex Barsky and Zack Lydon at Zine Hug. Any video can be transformed into a risograph animation. This is the general process for how to create a riso animation:

  • Export video into an image sequence
  • Create a contact sheet from that image sequence
  • Print the contact sheet with the risograph printer
  • Scan the contact sheet
  • Re-sequence the printed images into a digital video
